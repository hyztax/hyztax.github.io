What is Tax Hood?

Tax Hood is a Discord server owned by Hyztax. In here you can play games, engage in discussions, share experiences, and watch livestreams with alot of other members

What language is used in Tax Hood??

Tax Hood is primarily conducted in Swedish. However, our community is inclusive and welcomes members from all backgrounds, regardless of their proficiency in Swedish. Language barriers are not a hindrance to participation or engagement.

Can I join Tax Hood if I don't understand Swedish?

Absolutely! While Swedish is the main language used, we encourage individuals from all linguistic backgrounds to join our community.

What can I expect from Tax Hood?

Tax Hood offers a welcoming and fun enviroment to chat with other servermembers interested in the same topics as you and of course engage in the activitys wihtin the server
