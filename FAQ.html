What is Taxy?

Taxy is a Discord server owned by Hyztax. In here you can play games, engage in discussions, share experiences, and watch livestreams with alot of other members

What language is used in Taxy??

Taxy is primarily conducted in Swedish. However, our community is inclusive and welcomes members from all backgrounds, regardless of their proficiency in Swedish. Language barriers are not a hindrance to participation or engagement.

Can I join Taxy if I don't understand Swedish?

Absolutely! While Swedish is the main language used, we encourage individuals from all linguistic backgrounds to join our community.
